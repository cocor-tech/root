# Agency Services

The Cocor Tech Agency builds software that moves businesses forward. We offer four core services:

## Custom Software Development

Full-stack applications, APIs, internal tools, and platforms built to your specifications. We handle architecture, development, deployment, and maintenance.

**Typical engagement**: $20K - $200K project-based.

## MVP Development

From idea to working product in 4-12 weeks. Rapid iteration with validated learning. We help you test product-market fit before committing to a full build.

**Typical engagement**: $10K - $50K fixed-price.

## Staff Augmentation

Senior engineers integrated into your existing team. We work on your stack, your schedule, your tools. Monthly retainer with full-time dedication.

**Typical engagement**: $5K - $30K/month.

## Legacy Modernization

Upgrade aging systems to modern stacks. Reduce technical debt, improve performance, and enable faster feature development. Risk-managed migration approach.

**Typical engagement**: $30K - $150K project-based.

## Our Stack

Next.js, React, TypeScript, Go, Rust, Stellar/Soroban, PostgreSQL, Redis, Docker, Tailwind CSS, and more depending on project needs.

## Delivery

We operate from Nigeria and deliver globally. Our low cost base means competitive pricing without compromising quality.
