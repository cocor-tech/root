# Getting Started

Cocor Tech operates three divisions. How you get started depends on which one you want to engage with.

## Agency Clients

If you need custom software built:

1. Visit our [Contact](/contact) page and submit a project brief
2. We'll respond within 48 hours with a proposal
3. Discovery phase: we define scope, architecture, and timeline
4. Development begins with 2-week sprints
5. You receive regular demos and progress updates

## Product Users

Our flagship product Moistello is live at [moistello.com](https://moistello.com). To get started:

1. Create a passkey with device key/ biometrics
2. Browse or create a savings circle
3. Set your contribution amount and frequency
4. Invite members or join an existing circle

## Brand Asset Sellers

If you own a domain or digital property you'd like to sell:

- Review our [acquisition criteria](/brands/acquisition-criteria)
- Reach out via our [contact form](/contact) with details
- We'll respond with an offer or pass
