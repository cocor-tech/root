# Hiring Remote Developers from Nigeria: What to Know

Nigeria has one of the largest and fastest-growing software engineering workforces in the world. Here is what Western companies should know.

## The Talent Pool

Nigeria produces tens of thousands of computer science graduates annually. The engineering community is active, English-speaking, and experienced with modern stacks. Nigerian engineers work across fintech, blockchain, SaaS, and enterprise software.

## Cost Advantage

The cost of living in Nigeria is significantly lower than in North America or Europe. This translates to competitive rates for engineering talent without sacrificing quality.

## Time Zone

Nigeria is in West Africa Time (UTC+1), which overlaps with both European (3-4 hours) and North American (5-6 hours) business hours. This makes real-time collaboration feasible.

## Communication

English is the official language of Nigeria. Nigerian engineers communicate fluently in written and spoken English, reducing the friction common in other outsourcing destinations.

## What to Watch For

- **Internet reliability** — Ensure backup connectivity is in place
- **Payment infrastructure** — Use stable payment methods (Payoneer, crypto, etc.)
- **Equipment** — Provide budget for quality hardware
