# MoiScore: Building On-Chain Reputation for the Unbanked

MoiScore is Moistello's on-chain reputation system — a portable, transparent, and fair credit score for the unbanked.

## The Problem

1.7 billion adults have no formal credit history. When they join a savings circle, there is no data to assess their reliability. Traditional ROSCAs rely on personal trust, which limits scale.

## The Solution: MoiScore

MoiScore is a 0-1000 score calculated entirely from on-chain behavior:

- **Streak** (35%) — Consecutive on-time payments
- **Completions** (30%) — Successful circle completions
- **Volume** (20%) — Total contributions across all circles
- **Recency** (15%) — Recent activity level

## Score Tiers

| Range | Tier | Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| 0-200 | Bronze | Basic access |
| 201-400 | Silver | Higher circle limits |
| 401-600 | Gold | Premium circles |
| 601-800 | Platinum | Governance voting |
| 801-1000 | Diamond | Zero collateral |

## Why On-Chain?

MoiScore lives on the Stellar blockchain, not in a private database. This means:

- **Portable** — Take your score to any circle, any platform
- **Transparent** — Anyone can verify the calculation
- **Immutable** — History cannot be erased or manipulated
- **Permissionless** — No gatekeepers

## The Vision

MoiScore is our first step toward becoming a reputation protocol. Eventually, any lender, platform, or service could check a user's MoiScore to make trust decisions — without requiring a traditional credit bureau.
