# Why We Built Moistello on Stellar

When we started building Moistello, we evaluated multiple blockchain platforms. We chose Stellar for several reasons that align directly with our mission.

## Sub-Cent Fees

Stellar transaction fees are fractions of a cent. For savings circles where members may contribute small amounts regularly, high gas fees would make the system uneconomical. Stellar's fee model makes micro-savings viable.

## Fast Settlement

Transactions on Stellar settle in 3-5 seconds. When a circle member needs to make a contribution before a deadline, waiting minutes or hours for confirmation is unacceptable. Stellar's speed enables real-time group finance.

## Soroban Smart Contracts

Stellar's Soroban platform allows us to write smart contracts in Rust — a language we already use and trust. The Soroban SDK is well-designed, well-documented, and purpose-built for financial applications.

## Mission Alignment

Stellar's mission is to connect the world's financial infrastructure and make money move like email. Moistello's mission is to bring savings and credit to the 1.7 billion unbanked adults. The alignment is natural.

## The Result

Moistello is a trustless, low-cost, fast savings platform built on a blockchain designed for global financial inclusion. Every design decision — from fee structure to wallet support — reflects this foundation.
