# The Cocor Tech Flywheel

Cocor Tech operates through three divisions. They are not separate businesses — they are a single flywheel where each division amplifies the others.

## The Three Divisions

**Products** — We build and operate digital products. Our flagship is Moistello, a decentralized savings platform on Stellar. Products are the visible face of Cocor Tech.

**Agency** — We build custom software for companies worldwide. Enterprise quality at competitive rates, delivered from Nigeria.

**Brand Assets** — We acquire premium domains and digital properties, develop them into valuable assets, and hold or exit at multiples.

## How the Flywheel Works

```
Agency Revenue → Funds Product R&D
  → Products Grow → Brand Value Increases
    → Brand Assets Acquired with Capital
      → Developed & Flipped for Profit
        → Capital Returns to Company
          → More Agency Capability
```

Agency revenue funds product development without outside capital. Successful products increase the Cocor Tech brand value. Brand value helps acquire better brand assets. Profits from brand asset exits return to the company, funding more agency capability and product R&D.

## Why This Works

The flywheel model de-risks each division individually:

- The agency generates predictable cash flow
- Products build long-term brand and IP value
- Brand assets provide outsized returns on strategic acquisitions

No single division carries the entire company. Each one supports the others.

## The Long Game

We measure success in decades, not quarters. The flywheel compounds over time — each cycle makes the next one more powerful.
