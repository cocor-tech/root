# About Cocor Tech

Cocor Tech is an operating company that builds, acquires, and scales digital assets for profit. We operate through three distinct divisions — Products, Agency, and Brand Assets — each reinforcing the others in a compounding flywheel.

## Our Mission

To build, acquire, and scale digital assets that create lasting value. We believe the greatest opportunities in technology come not from a single product, but from the ability to identify, build, and operate multiple digital assets simultaneously.

## The Three Divisions

**Products** — We build and operate digital products. Our flagship is Moistello, a decentralized savings platform on the Stellar blockchain. Products are the visible face of Cocor Tech.

**Agency** — We build custom software for companies worldwide. Operating from Nigeria with global pricing, we deliver enterprise-quality engineering at competitive rates. Agency revenue funds product research and development.

**Brand Assets** — We acquire premium domains and digital properties, develop them into valuable assets, and hold or exit at multiples. This is our long-term wealth-building engine.

## The Founder

Cocor Tech was founded by Nekwachukwu Ucheokoye — a software engineer and entrepreneur building at the intersection of blockchain, fintech, and digital assets.

## Our Values

- **Transparency** — We say what we've built and what we're working toward.
- **Ambition** — We think in decades, not quarters.
- **Craftsmanship** — We build things that last.
- **Long-term thinking** — The best assets compound over time.
