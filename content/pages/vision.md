# Vision

Cocor Tech is building toward a future where digital assets are the primary store of value and vehicle for wealth creation. Our three-division structure is designed to compound over time.

## Products

Moistello is our first product. It is a decentralized savings and credit platform for the 1.7 billion unbanked adults worldwide. Built on the Stellar blockchain, it offers trustless savings circles, on-chain reputation scoring, and sub-cent fees.

Future products will expand into adjacent financial infrastructure — always with the same principles: decentralized, accessible, and useful.

## Agency

The agency serves two purposes: it generates revenue that funds product development, and it keeps our engineering team sharp by solving diverse client problems. Over time, the agency will transition from a primary revenue driver to a strategic capability.

## Brand Assets

This is our moat. We identify undervalued digital properties — premium domains, established brands, niche businesses — and develop them into significant assets. We hold, grow, and strategically exit at multiples.

## The Long Game

We measure success in decades, not quarters. Each division feeds the others. Agency revenue funds product R&D. Products increase brand value. Brand assets generate capital that returns to the company. The flywheel compounds.
