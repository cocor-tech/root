# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how Cocor Tech collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

## Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

- **Contact information** — Name and email address when you contact us or submit a form.
- **Usage data** — Anonymous analytics data about how you interact with our website.
- **Cookies** — Essential cookies for site functionality. No tracking cookies.

## How We Use Your Information

- To respond to your inquiries
- To improve our website and services
- To send occasional updates (only if you opt in)

## Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services. Analytics are privacy-focused (no personal data collected). Contact forms are processed through secure third-party providers.

## Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. We do not sell your data to third parties.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact: hi@cocor.tech
